Research

We did a full audit of Club Closer's current funnel, marketing, and market positioning. Here are the key findings:

  • Risk Opt-in page has 10+ form fields. This is killing conversion rates. Best practice is 2 to 3 fields maximum for a webinar registration.
  • Gap No webinar funnel exists. The current path sends cold traffic straight to a long application form with no warming step in between.
  • Gap No email nurture sequences. Leads who do not convert immediately are lost with no follow-up system.
  • Opportunity 103 Trustpilot reviews (4.8 stars) are not featured anywhere in the funnel. This is a massive proof stack sitting unused.
  • Opportunity No YouTube presence. Competitors in the remote closing space are building audiences through YouTube content. This is an open channel.

VSL Outline
Script

A 9-section video sales letter outline covering the remote closing opportunity. This is the framework for a webinar or long-form video that warms prospects before they apply.

01
Hook / Pattern Interrupt
Open with the income claim that stops the scroll. Lead with the outcome, not the opportunity name.
02
Problem / Agitation
Name the pain of the 9-to-5 grind, failed side hustles, and the feeling of trading time for a capped salary.
03
Solution Introduction
Introduce remote closing as the mechanism. Explain why high-ticket sales is the highest-paid skill most people overlook.
04
Credibility
Marcus's origin story. From bartending to building a multi-million pound training company. 450+ students trained.
05
Benefits and Outcomes
Work from anywhere. Uncapped commissions. No need to build a product, brand, or following. Get paid to close deals for other businesses.
06
Social Proof
Student results, Trustpilot reviews, and specific case studies showing income earned after completing the programme.
07
The Offer
Walk through what Club Closer includes. Training modules, live coaching, placement support, and the community.
08
Guarantee / Risk Reversal
Address the fear of investing. Position the guarantee around support and placement to remove the "what if it does not work" objection.
09
Close / Call to Action
Direct them to apply. Restate the opportunity cost of waiting. One clear next step.
